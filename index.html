About Me

My research interests include AI for Software Engineering, Human Factors in Software Engineering, Human Factors in Cybersecurity, LLMs and Generative AI, and Software Engineering Education and Training. I am currently advised by my great mentor, Dr. Kevin Leach. You can view other people whom I have worked with on the "People" tab above.

I have a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Vanderbilt University, where I was also a grader and AV assistant. My undergraduate research focused on remote mental health applications for young students recovering from the COVID-19 pandemic. In my professional experience, I have further worked as an IT and Cybersecurity Intern for CarTech US.
